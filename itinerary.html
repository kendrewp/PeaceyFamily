Trip Itinerary

Our 24-day adventure through South Africa

Day 1

Departure

Depart Denver/DC

Day 2

Arrival in Cape Town

Arrive Cape Town - Evening

Welcome to Cape Town - Table Mountain awaits!

Day 3

V&A Waterfront & Robben Island

Explore the lively V&A Waterfront: shopping, entertainment, Two Oceans Aquarium, or harbor cruise. Evening stroll and dinner. Ferry tour (book early) to the historic prison site, guided by former inmates.

The iconic V&A Waterfront and historic Robben Island

Day 4

Table Mountain & Signal Hill

Cable car to Table Mountain (book ahead; hike if weather permits). Panoramic views. Sunset at Signal Hill.

Spectacular views from Table Mountain

Day 5

Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens

Explore UNESCO-listed Kirstenbosch: fynbos trails, Boomslang walkway, picnic spots.

The famous Boomslang walkway at Kirstenbosch

Day 6

Bo-Kaap, City Center & Castle

Morning walking tour of colorful Bo-Kaap for photos and Cape Malay culture. Afternoon: Company's Garden, museums, or Castle of Good Hope.

The vibrant colors of Bo-Kaap and historic city center

Day 7

Abseiling Table Mountain

You get some serious bragging rights when Abseiling down one of the 7 wonders of nature which is the iconic Table Mountain.

Thrilling abseil down the iconic Table Mountain

Day 8

Chapman's Peak Drive & Hout Bay & Seal Island boat trip

Drive the spectacular Chapman's Peak. Stop in Hout Bay for harbor and markets.

Chapman's Peak Drive and Seal Island adventure

Day 9

Boulders Beach, Simon's Town & Cape Point

Full peninsula day: funicular to lighthouse, hikes, wildlife, iconic signpost, African penguins at Boulders Beach. Explore naval Simon's Town.

Full peninsula day: Penguins, naval history, and the legendary Cape of Good Hope

Day 10

Kalk Bay & Muizenberg

Harbor, galleries, colorful huts, seafood.

Day 11

Winelands (Stellenbosch & Franschhoek)

Base here (~1hr drive). Tastings in Stellenbosch, Wine Tram in Franschhoek, gourmet meals.

The beautiful Winelands and the famous Franschhoek Wine Tram

Day 12

Hermanus Whale Watching, Cape Agulhas

Drive to Hermanus trip for land-based or boat whale viewing (peak early season). Drive to Cape Agulhas, drive to Oudtshoorn, overnight.

Spectacular whale watching in Hermanus

Day 13

Oudtshoorn

Drive back to Hermanus. Overnight.

Famous ostrich farms in Oudtshoorn

Day 14

Shark Dive

Drive back to Cape Town, after shark dive.

Thrilling shark cage diving adventure

Day 15

Zeitz MOCAA & Silo District

Africa's largest contemporary art museum in iconic silo building.

The stunning Zeitz Museum of Contemporary Art Africa

Day 16

Soar off Lion's Head or Signal Hill for breathtaking aerial views of the city, Atlantic seaboard, and Table Mountain

Soar above Cape Town with stunning aerial views
